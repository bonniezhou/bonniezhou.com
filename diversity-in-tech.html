We belong in tech.

This doesn't really need explaining. There are a million and one reasons why diversity is important in the tech industry, and why it's currently missing.

Women belong in tech. Black folk belong in tech. Latinx folk belong in tech. Native Americans belong in tech. Queer folk belong in tech. Trans folk belong in tech. Beginners belong in tech. If you feel like an outsider or impostor in tech, I've been there and I see you. Tech needs you. We need you. They need us.
